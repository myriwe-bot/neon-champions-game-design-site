## Story goal

Implement / verify the post-use readability smoke for the existing prototype Champion Operation.

After spending Intel on `prototype_operation_site_forecast`, the player must understand:

- which site was forecasted;
- that 1 Intel was spent;
- that the forecast marker exists;
- that repeating the same operation target is denied without spending more Intel or mutating other markers;
- that ordinary strategic movement/site/tactical interaction remains unbroken around the marker.
