Story contract:
- Human playtest says objective capture is too easy and can be “snuck”.
- Implement the smallest real fix: central objective capture/hold must be visibly multi-turn.
- Align with Strategic Map §13: central objective hold victory requires 2 consecutive own-turn checks by default, stored as scenario/state data rather than hardcoded immediate victory.
- The same faction must still control/hold the objective at a later own-turn check before objective victory resolves.

In scope:
- Runtime state/support for central-objective hold progress:
  - central objective site ID;
  - holding/controlling faction ID;
  - current hold progress;
  - required hold progress, default 2.
- Strategic rules so objective interaction/control starts or advances visible hold state instead of immediate same-interaction victory.
- Start-of-own-turn objective progress and victory evaluation.
- Reset/interruption behavior when the opposing faction takes or contests the objective before completion.
- Strategic HUD/status/result text showing:
  - controlling/holding faction;
  - current progress and required progress;
  - turns/checks remaining;
  - opponent counterplay window;
  - why progress reset or did not advance.
- Tests and PlayMode/evidence for countdown, reset/interruption, and final victory after required progress.

Out of scope:
- No round-based defense waves.
- No multi-objective control-points system.
- No score/race victory mode.
- No new tactical battle modes, tactical objective redesign, or tactical AI behavior.
- No strategic AI.
- No fog/stealth/hidden objective state.
- No new map layout, final art, icons, VFX, audio, or animation.
- No campaign persistence/save-load format expansion beyond serializable state fields needed here.
- No Champion recovery/revival/capture rules.
- No broader economy, Intel, Operations, Command, recruitment, or faction-specific objective rules.

Allowed placeholders / assumptions:
- Use clear prototype UI text, not final lore copy.
- Use existing strategic HUD/status/result presentation surfaces where practical.
- Existing central objective site may be used with default required hold count 2.
- If the current runtime lacks a full `contested` state, implement the smallest equivalent reset/interruption behavior and document the limitation in evidence; do not invent a full control-points model.

Acceptance criteria:
- Capturing/interacting with the central objective no longer wins immediately from the same interaction alone.
- Objective hold progress is recorded and visible for the acting/controlling faction.
- Start-of-own-turn objective checks advance progress and only win when required progress is met.
- `objectiveHoldRequired` is data/state driven for the scenario, with working default 2.
- Opposing control/contest before completion resets prior progress and explains the reset/interruption in feedback.
- Champion defeat victory remains immediate priority after battle/result application.
- Strategic HUD/status/result feedback shows holder/controller, progress, remaining checks/turns, and counterplay window.
- Invalid or unavailable objective interactions explain why and do not partially mutate hidden objective progress.
- Existing objective, Champion encounter, guarded-site, command explanation, and loop smoke behavior is not regressed outside the approved objective timing change.
