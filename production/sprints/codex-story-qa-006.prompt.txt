Story contract:
- This is a player-facing readability repair across strategic and tactical modes.
- Preserve the human playtest complaints as the source of truth:
  - unclear active faction / turn / turn changes;
  - unclear selected Champion location on some nodes;
  - unclear guarded/site/objective state;
  - unclear tactical current stack and friendly/enemy identity;
  - legal attacks/targets look like selecting the other army;
  - attack/damage/result feedback is not understood;
  - Champion commands are visible but not explained enough;
  - UX blockers: clickable affordances, denial reasons, result feedback, battle/action understanding.

In scope:
- Strategic HUD/panel/text/marker improvements for active faction, turn/round, selected Champion, active Champion, location, reachable routes/nodes, guarded/controlled/objective states.
- Strategic result/denial feedback for movement, site interaction, battle result return, and end turn.
- Tactical HUD/panel/text/marker improvements for tactical mode, current side/current active stack, friendly/enemy identity, legal move destinations, legal attack targets.
- Tactical result/denial feedback for move, attack, pass/wait/defend, battle result, and next active stack.
- Short explanatory text for existing Rally Order and Drone Strike behavior/cost/target/denials.
- Presentation/application/domain helper changes only as needed to expose existing state cleanly; cover with tests.

Out of scope:
- No new mechanics, economy, objective rules, capture rules, retaliation rules, AI, pathfinding, or encounter rules.
- No map redesign away from the current node graph.
- No multi-turn objective capture.
- No Champion-vs-Champion encounter trigger redesign; reserved for STORY-QA-007.
- No new command effects, Command economy, cooldowns, progression, skill trees, or full Operations UI.
- No new units, attack types, creature depth, final balance, final art, icons, VFX, audio, animation, portraits, or final lore copy.
- No save/load or persistence expansion.
- If a clarity fix requires a gameplay rule change, stop and report the blocker instead of expanding scope.

Acceptance criteria:
- Given the strategic map is loaded, visible UI clearly shows active faction, round/turn, selected Champion, active Champion, and selected Champion location.
- Given a guarded, unguarded, controlled, or objective site is visible, player-facing text/markers distinguish those states.
- Given a legal or illegal strategic action occurs, feedback says what happened or why it was denied.
- Given End Turn succeeds, feedback states previous and new active faction/turn/round enough that time advancement is clear.
- Given tactical mode is active, current side/current stack/friendly/enemy identity are visibly distinct.
- Given tactical legal moves/attacks exist, legal move destinations and legal attack targets are visible and not confused with selection ownership.
- Given an attack succeeds, feedback states attacker, target, damage, remaining target count or defeat, and next active side/stack or battle result.
- Given an action/command is denied, feedback states a concrete reason and does not mutate state.
- Given Rally Order or Drone Strike is visible, the UI explains what the command does in prototype terms and what it costs/targets.
- Existing tactical command mechanics and outcomes remain unchanged except for player-facing clarity.
